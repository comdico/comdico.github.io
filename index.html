Websites tell stories about people, brands, and organizations. Authentic stories have the power to move us. So we build the experience collaboratively, from the inside out: Vision, Content, and Mobile first.

In the heat of a busy project, with rushed schedules, tight budgets, and complex requirements, it is all-too easy to lose sight of the Big Picture. Effective User Experience Design maintains a project's original vision and sees it through to final delivery.

I believe that User Experience Design can, and should, retain an artisanal approach, one where the spine of the product is not only minimally viable, but also deeply cohesive. Furthermore, it is interesting that collaboration is the key that unlocks a project's true potential.

There is a proven methodology to achieve high quality results - and is used to produce most motion pictures. Interestingly, our industry's techniques, however less glamorous, turn out to be very similar to film making.

For example:

Persona : Cast
Waypoints : Acts
Usability Test : Screen Test
Wireframe : Script
Prototype : Storyboard
Scenarios : Backstory
Storymap : Plot
End Users : Audience
Collaboration : Collaboration
